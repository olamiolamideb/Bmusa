About Me


Early Life and Education

Pastor Benjamin Audu Musa was born on the 8th of March, 1974, in Kogi State, Nigeria. Raised in northern Nigeria, he developed a strong foundation in faith and education. He pursued higher education at Usmanu Danfodiyo University, where he earned a Master's degree in Peace and Security Studies and a Postgraduate Diploma in Public Administration. In addition, he has multiple Postgraduate Diplomas in Christian Leadership. He was awarded an honorary Doctorate of Divinity from Yeshua International School of Theology, London.


Ministry and Work

Benjamin Audu Musa is an ordained minister of the gospel with Word Integrity Ministries, Christ House Church, Sokoto. He was ordained as a pastor on the 5th of December, 2000. Known for his strong prophetic teaching grace, Ben Musa's ministry is characterized by signs and wonders. He is a passionate advocate of Kingdom-based prayers and has dedicated his life to missionary work. He currently serves as the regional pastor of the ministry, overseeing various church activities and outreach programs. In addition to his pastoral duties, Ben Musa hosts the radio program Rhema Power Network. This program broadcasts in both Hausa and English, reaching a diverse audience in Sokoto and Kebbi States. His teachings and messages have impacted many lives, fostering spiritual growth and development in the region.


Personal Life

Benjamin Audu Musa is married to Pastor Bentonia Ene Audu, who is also a dedicated servant in the ministry. Together, they work tirelessly in the vineyard, upporting and uplifting their congregation. Their union is blessed with five biological children, and they have also opened their hearts and home to adopted children, embodying the true spirit of Christian love and generosity.


Leadership Roles

Dr. Ben Musa holds significant leadership positions within the Christian community. He is the current State Chairman of the Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria, Sokoto, and serves as the regional secretary of the same organization. His leadership and guidance have been instrumental in uniting and strengthening the Pentecostal community in the region. In addition to these roles, he is involved in various responsibilities and initiatives aimed at promoting the body of Christ.
