About
Pastor Ben Musa

Pastor Benjamin Audu Musa serves as the state pastor and chairman of the Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria (PFN) in Sokoto. As the pastor in charge of Christ House, Pastor Musa aims to bring transformative change and provide effective, pragmatic, and result-oriented leadership to the Pentecostal community in Sokoto and beyond.

This time, my pregnancy journey was smooth, and on July 16, 2023, I safely delivered my baby after less than 3 hours of active labor. Glory to God!.
